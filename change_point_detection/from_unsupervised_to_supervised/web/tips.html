• Indicate the homogeneous segments of the time series: click with the left button of the mouse where you think the statistical properties (mean, variance, etc) have changed. A red line will be placed where you click. Classification example
  • You can remove the change points with the button "Remove last change point".
  • In case of doubts about a statistical change, you can navigate through different scales and check if your uncertainty persists. You can click in any scale.
  • Outlier is not a statistical change. An outlier is an observation that lies outside the overall pattern of a distribution. Outlier example
  • Don't forget to click in "Save and next".
  • Your e-mail is only used to identify you in the system, avoiding that you check the same time series more than once.
